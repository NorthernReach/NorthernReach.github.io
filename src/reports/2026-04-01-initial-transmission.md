## Purpose

This site serves as a central repository for field reports, project documentation, and technical notes. Content is published as static files — no databases, no trackers, no frameworks beyond what's needed to render text to screen.

## Format

Reports are authored in Markdown and built with Eleventy. The aesthetic is intentional — monospace type, box-drawing borders, ANSI color palettes. Information should look like information.
